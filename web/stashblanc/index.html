stashblanc is an artistic visualization of aesthetic 'randomness'. two gifs, sourced from gyphy, are chosen every four seconds and put together to music. the large array of images and fitting soundtrack blend to present a continious set of arbitrary combinations that's easy to get lost in watching - ultimately creating an exploration into the 'art of random'.

presented and developed by luka song. my project was inspired by cachemonet. emotional support provided by vivi. open source and changelog on github.
